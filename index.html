SOBRE MI

If you got here, I assume you are interested in knowing a little more about my personal and professional profile. First of all, I want you to know that I already have a university degree, I am a Lawyer graduated from the National University of La Plata. That said, you may be wondering why I entered the IT field. The truth is that I have always been passionate about technology in general, I firmly believe in the creative and transformative power of technological tools. After several years of indecision, finally in the year 2022, I decided to change the course of my life, with all the risks that this entails, but with the conviction of being sure of myself about what I aspired to. I did not want to work more in relation to my profession, but on the contrary, I longed to begin to insert myself professionally in the IT area, for which I began to study beforehand and to take all kinds of courses related to it. As of today, I already have a Web Development course at the Coder House academy, which I successfully passed with a final grade of 9; there I made a final project based on an e-commerce of natural cosmetics, having used for it, HTML, CSS, SASS, and BOOTSTRAP. I am currently taking the JavaScript course.
I would love for you to give me the opportunity to have my first job in the IT field as a Frontend developer or to train me if you think it is necessary for this purpose. In me you will find an autonomous, self-taught, responsible person with a great desire to learn. I feel very comfortable working in a team as well as independently.
